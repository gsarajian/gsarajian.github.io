Welcome to my website. I recently completed my PhD in Mathematics at the University of California, Santa Barbara under Yitang Zhang. I like thinking about numbers and the stories they hide. My theoretical math research interests are in analytic number theory, and my applied math interests are in data science, cryptography and machine learning. I plan to update this website with more of my projects, both applied and theoretical, so stay tuned!
